About Lung Cancer Detection System

Project Overview

This Lung Cancer Detection System is an advanced deep learning-based application designed to assist in the early detection of lung cancer through CT scan image analysis.

Leveraging state-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), our system can accurately classify lung CT scans into three categories: Normal, Benign, and Malignant, helping healthcare professionals make more informed decisions.
